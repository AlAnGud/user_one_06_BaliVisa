How it works

Currently, the only visa you can obtain to enter Indonesia remotely is the B211A. This is a single entry electronic visa for humanitarian work, volunteering, family reunion, business or investment visit.

This e-VISA Allows you to stay up to 60 days, If you plan to stay more than 60 days this visa can be extended 4 Times. Each extension with 30 days.

Following Indonesian regulations due Covid 19, they are giving exemptions for foreigners to enter Indonesia with B211A Single Entry e-Visa for very specific Reason Such as Business Visit
